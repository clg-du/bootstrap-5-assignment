Course content

What you will learn in this course

Chapter 1 - Introduction to Bootstrap 5

Duration: 1 hour

Level: Beginner

This chapter covers the basics of Bootstrap 5 and how to get started with the framework.

Chapter 2 - The Grid System

Duration: 1 hour

Level: Intermediate

This chapter covers the grid system in Bootstrap 5 and how to use it to create responsive layouts.

Chapter 3 - Customizing Bootstrap

Duration: 1 hour

Level: Advanced

This chapter covers how to customize Bootstrap 5 to fit your needs and create your own themes.

Chapter 4 - Advanced Components

Duration: 1 hour

Level: Advanced

This chapter covers some of the more advanced components in Bootstrap 5, such as modals, carousels, and tooltips.

Duration: 1 hour

Level: Advanced
